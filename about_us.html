Welcome to the Smart Archive, a pioneering web-based archival system designed specifically for the Eastern Visayas State University - Carigara Campus (EVSU-CC) Theses collection. Our platform is dedicated to preserving and providing access to the rich academic heritage of EVSU-CC through advanced digital archiving technologies.

Mission

Our mission is to create a comprehensive and user-friendly repository that ensures the longevity and accessibility of EVSU-CC theses. By leveraging innovative web technologies, we aim to facilitate seamless discovery, retrieval, and utilization of scholarly works produced by the university's students and researchers.

Vision

We envision the Smart Archive as a vital resource for academic research, supporting both current and future generations of scholars, educators, and professionals. Through continuous enhancement and adaptation of archival practices, we strive to maintain the highest standards of digital preservation and information integrity.

Features

  • Digital Preservation: Safeguarding the intellectual output of EVSU-CC through robust digital preservation techniques.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Providing an intuitive interface for effortless browsing, searching, and accessing thesis documents.
  • Accessibility: Ensuring accessibility compliance to accommodate diverse user needs and preferences.
  • Collaborative Platform: Fostering collaboration among researchers and educators by promoting the sharing of knowledge and ideas.
