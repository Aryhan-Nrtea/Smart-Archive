Welcome to the Smart Archive, a groundbreaking web-based archival system dedicated to preserving and showcasing the intellectual wealth of the Eastern Visayas State University - Carigara Campus (EVSU-CC). Our platform is designed to revolutionize access to and preservation of EVSU-CC theses, fostering a digital repository that ensures the enduring legacy of academic research and scholarly achievement.

What We Offer

  • Digital Preservation: Utilizing state-of-the-art digital archiving techniques to safeguard the integrity and accessibility of EVSU-CC theses.

  • Ease of Access: Offering a seamless and intuitive interface for browsing, searching, and retrieving thesis documents, enhancing accessibility for researchers, educators, and students alike.

  • Collaboration and Engagement: Facilitating collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers, promoting academic dialogue and innovation.
